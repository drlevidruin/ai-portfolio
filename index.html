The story that started it

We saved a teaching line. The kids jumped a grade level in math.

The problem

We could not afford another assistant teacher for math. Kids needing extra practice were falling behind.

What we did

Went all-in on HMH Waggle K–8. 120 students, 6 teachers. Wired growth into our SIS. Equipped, didn't hire.
