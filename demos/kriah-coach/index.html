✨ How Kriah Coach works

  • Daily Each kriah session logs fluency (words per minute) and per-letter accuracy from a tap interface — fast enough to use during a 5-minute reading check.
  • Weekly Claude reads the fluency trend, workbook progress, and recent teacher notes, then writes a one-paragraph coaching insight with a specific next step.
  • Monthly Parents get an auto-drafted update in family-friendly language, ready for the teacher to review, edit, and send.
Built on: Apps Script · Google Sheets · Claude · the same stack that powers the Pardes SIS Dashboard. Everything stays inside the Google Workspace the school already uses.
