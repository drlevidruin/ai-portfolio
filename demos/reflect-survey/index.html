Hidden strengths

Mutual growth areas

Rated low by both self and others

  • Direction-setting in curriculum decisions (T4.1): 3.9 overall · 3.7 self
  • Documenting decision rationale so staff understand the "why"

Keep · Start · Stop

Synthesized recommendations grounded in open-ended responses and rating patterns.

Keep

  • The "culture of warmth" staff describe — keep holding the weekly staff tefillah circle
  • Continue personal birthday notes to each teacher; multiple respondents named this unprompted

Start

  • A monthly written newsletter to families to tighten communication rhythm
  • Document decision rationales in 1-paragraph notes so staff understand the "why" before changes land

Stop

  • Stop letting Friday announcements run past 15 minutes — multiple notes on length
  • Hold off on adding new initiatives until current ones have full teacher buy-in
