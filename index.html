The story that started everything

We saved a teaching line. The kids jumped a grade level in math.

Math · 2025–2026

We chose to equip, not to hire. The results came back fast.

The problem

A small school could not afford another full-time assistant teacher for math support. Kids who needed extra practice were falling behind, and hiring more staff was not an option.

What we did

We went all-in on HMH Waggle for adaptive math practice K–8, licensed 120 students and 6 teachers, and wired it into our own student information system so teachers could track growth in one place. Instead of hiring, we equipped.

Inside the SIS

What is live today, and what we are building next.

Live · Every teacher

Dashboard + Profiles

Student counts by grade and gender, click into a full profile with family info, attendance history, report card PDFs, and color-coded principal report history.

Live · Every day

Attendance V2

Google Forms-based attendance intake with tardy detection. Teachers submit daily, results feed the SIS instantly. Parent tardy emails ready to enable when the policy is approved.

Live · All staff

Staff Directory

Staff records, clock-in and clock-out data, PTO tracking, and HR notes. Role-based: admins see everything, teachers see only themselves and their class.

Live · K-5

Principal Reports

Structured principal observations archived as PDFs in Drive. Color-coded history per student so patterns jump out over multiple years.

What comes next

This is just the beginning.

The goal was never the tech. It was a better education for every child. What you just saw is a year of quiet building, after hours, for one reason: the kids deserve the same caliber of tools a startup in Silicon Valley gets to use. If one principal with a laptop and Claude can do this, every small organization can do a version of it.
