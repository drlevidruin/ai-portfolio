The story that started everything

We saved a teaching line. The kids jumped a grade level in math.

Math · 2025–2026

We chose to equip, not to hire. The results came back fast.

The problem

A small school could not afford another full-time assistant teacher for math support. Kids who needed extra practice were falling behind, and hiring more staff was not an option.

What we did

We went all-in on HMH Waggle for adaptive math practice K–8, licensed 120 students and 6 teachers, and wired it into our own student information system so teachers could track growth in one place. Instead of hiring, we equipped.

Kriah Coach — Hebrew Reading Tracker

A per-child Hebrew reading tracker. Each week Claude reads the fluency trend, workbook progress, and teacher notes, then writes a one-paragraph coaching nudge. 22-letter mastery grid, workbook milestones from Pre-Aleph to Aleph Unit 3, fluency chart, AI insight card. Built on the same Apps Script foundation as the SIS.

What comes next

This is just the beginning.

The goal was never the tech. It was a better education for every child and a school that runs smoothly enough that the grown-ups can focus on kids. Here is what's on deck.

1

AI tutors for every subject

Kriah Coach was the start. Math, Chumash, English writing next. Per-child, grounded in what the teacher is teaching this week.

Voice AI that hears a child read and scores fluency live. Frees the teacher to coach instead of testing.

A portal that answers every parent question about their kid, their schedule, their teachers — in plain English, grounded in real school data.
